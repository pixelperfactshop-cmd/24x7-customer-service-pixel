About Us

Why choose Pixel Perfect for your TV repair needs

We've been perfecting TV repair for over 15 years

Pixel Perfect TV Repair was established in 2008 with a mission to provide high-quality, affordable TV repair services to our community. Our team of certified technicians has extensive experience with all major TV brands and types.

We pride ourselves on our quick response times, transparent pricing, and commitment to customer satisfaction. All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty for your peace of mind.

  • Certified and experienced technicians
  • 90-day warranty on all repairs
  • Same-day service available
  • Free diagnostic with repair
  • On-site and in-shop services
